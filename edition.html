edition

this diplomatic edition was developed by Delfina Sol Pandiani in 2019. Oxygen-XML software was used to create the transcriptions through XML-TEI encoding of the manuscripts.

the encoding of the texts focused on the rendering and comprehensability of Storni's processes of substitution. this is why in the transcriptions it is possible to see with different colors the additions and deletions that compose the substitutions. each of the subsistutions has been given a unique ID in the encoding, which allows users of the code to easily reference and computationally explore the nature of the substitutions.

i hope this will make the edition a useful tool for literature scholars. the effective rendering of these phenomena allows for a greater understanding of Storni's thinking and writing process.

furthermore, the inclusion of some drawings (such as arrows and lines) in the manuscript has been encoded through the use of the <figure> tag. learn more about the tags used in the tei-xml section of this site.
